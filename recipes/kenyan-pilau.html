Kenyan Pilau

This is the best Pilau for your family

An intricate and dangerous Pilau, invented by cookpad

Purpoted to make you feel like "having your brains smashed out by a spoon of Pilau"

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 3 blended tomatoes
  • Grated giner and garlic
  • Pre-boiled beef
  • Black pepper
  • Beef masala
  • Garam masala
  • Soy sauce
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Rice

Instructions

  1. Put one capful of cooking oil then put the two chopped onions and cook till the onions turn golden brown
  2. Blend the three tomatoes till pasty
  3. The preferred spices are as follows:
    • Garam masala
    • Beef masala
    • Garlic powder
    • Black pepper
  4. Put the blended tomatoes in cooking pot for 5-7 minutes
  5. Measure equal amounts of the previously mentioned spices in the cooking pot
  6. Put the spices in the cooking pot and stir
  7. Put the grated ginger and garlic in the cooking pot and stir
  8. Put the pre-boiled beef in the cooking pot and stir
  9. Cover the food with a lid and let it cook for 10-15 minutes
  10. Measure one and a half cups of rice
  11. Separate chaff from rice
  12. Wash and rinse rice twice
  13. Measure three cups of water and add to the cooking pot and wait for 5-10 minutes
  14. Use pilau masala and salt
  15. Add preferred amount of your choice of pilau masala and salt, then stir
  16. Put rice after the water has boiled and reduce heat and wait for 10 - 20 minutes
