Kenya Goat Meat Stew

The best stew to prepare for your loved ones

A mouth delicacy stew invented around the Kenyan coastal region.

It's sweetness and lightness will make your day.

Ingredients

  • Goat meat
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Ginger
  • Coriander
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Chop your meat into biteable size and wash....boil in water until....soft and tender
  2. Drain the excess water as you will use it as stew later on
  3. Add small amount of cooking oil into your pot with the meat and toss around until it turns somewhat golden brown
  4. Add in onions and toss some more before adding your crushed garlic and ginger paste
  5. Add in tomatoes and salt, and spices (optional) let it cook until the tomatoes are crushed..if it begins to stick onto the pot you can add some water
  6. Add in the stew you set aside and let it boil until you achieve your desired thickness
  7. Serve with an accompaniment of your choice
  8. I served mine with mash potatoes
